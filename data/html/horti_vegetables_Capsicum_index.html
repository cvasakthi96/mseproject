Capsicum (Sweet pepper / Bell pepper)
Capsicum annuum ; Solanaceae

Varieties

Arka Basant, Arka Gaurav, Arka Mohini, Green Gold, Bharath.

Soil

Well drained loamy soil rich in organic matter.

Season of sowing

September - February

Seed rate

  • Varieties : 1.25 kg/ha

  • Hybrids : 200 g / ha
