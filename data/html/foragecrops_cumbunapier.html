Package of Practices

1.

Season

:

Throughout the year under irrigated conditions in all districts

2.

Soil

:

All types of soils

3.

Preparatory cultivation

:

Plough 2 to 3 times to obtain a good tilth and form ridges and furrows.

4.

Manures and fertilizers

:

FYM : 25 t/ha
NPK : 150 : 50 : 40 kg/ha
Basal
FYM –25t/ha
NPK –75:50:40 Kg/ha
Top dressing
75 kg N/ha after 30 days after planting
75 kg N/ha after each cut

5.

Seed rate

:

33,333  two budded stem cuttings or rooted slips per ha.

6.

Spacing

:

60 x 50 cm

7.

Weed Management

:

Hand weeding whenever necessary

8.

Plant Protection

:

Generally not required

9.

Water Management

:

Immediately after planting; life irrigation on 3rd day and thereafter once in 10 days.

10.

Harvest

:

First harvest at 75-80 days after planting  and subsequent harvests once in 45 days

11.

Green fodder yield

:

375 - 400 t/ha/yr (7 harvests)
